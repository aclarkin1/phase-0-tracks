Liverpool Football Club ("LFC") is a professional soccer team based in the Northwest of England. LFC is the most successful soccer team in British history.

Among the competitions which Liverpool has won include:

  • The Champion's League
  • The UEFA Cup
  • The FA Cup
  • The League Cup
The top goal scorers in Liverpool's history are:
  1. Ian Rush
  2. Roger Hunt
  3. Gordon Hodgson
  4. Billy Liddell
